## Monster Statistics Explained

### AC (Armor Class)
Lower is better. AC 10 = no armor, AC 0 = plate mail + shield.

### HD (Hit Dice)
Number of d8 rolled for hit points. "+" adds to total.

### Move
Inches per turn (1" = 10 feet indoors, 10 yards outdoors).

### Attacks
Number and type of attacks per round.

### Special
Unique abilities, immunities, or weaknesses.

### XP
Experience points awarded for defeating the monster.

## Monster Types by Hit Dice

**1/2 HD**: Kobold
**1 HD**: Dwarf, Elf, Goblin, Hobgoblin, Orc, Skeleton
**1+ HD**: Bugbear, Gnoll, Lizard Man
**2 HD**: Ghoul, Ogre
**3 HD**: Harpy, Shadow
**4 HD**: Doppelganger, Gargoyle, Hell Hound, Wight
**5 HD**: Cockatrice, Ettercap, Hydra (5 heads), Mummy, Owlbear, Rust Monster, Wraith
**6 HD**: Basilisk, Carrion Crawler, Medusa, Minotaur, Ochre Jelly, Troll
**7 HD**: Chimera, Dragon (young), Griffon, Hydra (7 heads), Lich, Rakshasa, Vampire, Wyvern
**8 HD**: Demon (Type I), Giant (Hill), Golem (Flesh), Gorgon, Hydra (8 heads), Invisible Stalker, Umber Hulk, Xorn

## Special Abilities Reference

**Energy Drain**: Lose levels permanently (wight/wraith/spectre/vampire)
**Petrification**: Turned to stone (basilisk/cockatrice/gorgon/medusa)
**Poison**: Various effects, usually save or die
**Paralysis**: Cannot move (ghoul/carrion crawler)
**Regeneration**: Heal damage each round (troll)
**Magic Resistance**: Chance to ignore spells (demon/drow)
**Undead**: Immune to sleep/charm/hold, +1 or better to hit
