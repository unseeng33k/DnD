## THE COMPLETE ORCHESTRATION LOOP

```
PLAYER ACTION (Natural Language)
    ↓ [#6 ORCHESTRATOR]
    Parse intent in fiction
    
    ↓ [#8 SPOTLIGHT SCHEDULER]
    Check: Who needs spotlight? What's the pacing?
    
    ↓ [#7 WORLD-STATE GRAPH]
    Consult: Who exists? What relationships matter? What history?
    
    ↓ [#3 AGENCY]
    Confirm: This is a real choice. Player owns it.
    
    ↓ [#1 HEARTBEAT]
    Determine: What should happen? What are the stakes?

    Decide: Roll? If so, odds?

    Execute: Determine outcome.
    
    ↓ [#7 WORLD-STATE GRAPH]
    Log: Event. Update entities. Cascade consequences.
    
    ↓ [#8 SPOTLIGHT SCHEDULER]
    Record: What type of moment was this? Update fairness.
    
    ↓ [#5 LEGIBILITY]
    Narrate: Outcome clearly, vividly, without jargon.
    
    ↓ [#3 AGENCY]
    Track: Who drove this action? Who's now overdue for spotlight?
    
    ↓ PLAYER FEELS
    "I caused this. The world changed. I'm the protagonist.
     Others will get their turn too."
```
