# DM Soundtrack System

## Overview

Every environment, every encounter, every moment gets a soundtrack. Curated Spotify playlists, YouTube links, ambient generators — the full audio experience.

## How It Works

**During gameplay, the DM can:**
- Call up environment-appropriate music instantly
- Switch moods on the fly (exploration → combat → victory)
- Suggest specific tracks for key moments
- Build session playlists in advance

## Dynamic Playlist Building

**For a typical session:**

1. **Opening/Recap:** "Campfire Ambience" (soft, nostalgic)
2. **Travel/Exploration:** Environment-specific (jungle, dungeon, etc.)
3. **Discovery:** Slight tension increase, mysterious
4. **Combat:** Switch to high-energy combat music
5. **Post-Combat:** Brief victory sting, back to exploration
6. **The Reveal:** Horror/dread music for big moments
7. **Rest:** Peaceful, safe feeling
8. **Cliffhanger:** Tension building to end session

## The Rule

Music should enhance, not distract. If players are humming along instead of playing, it's too loud. If they forget it's there until a combat sting hits — perfect.
